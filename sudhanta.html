Questions, answered

Is the treatment painful?

Not at all. We use modern, gentle techniques and numbing options to keep every procedure comfortable and virtually pain-free. Your comfort is our priority.

What is included in the ₹99 Smile Check?

You receive a complete dental examination, digital intraoral photos, oral cancer screening, gum health assessment, cavity screening, smile analysis and a personalized treatment plan — all for ₹99, fully redeemable against any treatment within 30 days.

Just about 10 minutes. It's quick, thorough and easy to fit into a busy day — 10 minutes today for years of healthy smiles tomorrow.

Absolutely. We follow hospital-grade sterilization protocols, use single-use disposables where needed, and sanitize every treatment room between patients.
